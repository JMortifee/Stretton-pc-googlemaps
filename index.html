ABOUT STRETTON

Stretton is a large village on the outskirts of Burton on Trent in Staffordshire.

Stretton Parish has a population of close to 10,000 residents.

Stretton can boast residential, industrial and retail areas plus many green spaces, woodland walks and areas of interest.
